Playdates
Playdates are scheduled meetups where dog owners connect to let their dogs socialize, play, and exercise together in a safe and friendly environment. It’s an opportunity for dogs to make new furry friends, burn off energy, and learn social skills, while owners get to meet like-minded people and build a community of trusted pet lovers. For Happy Tails, playdates are at the core of what we offer: easy-to-arrange, fun, and safe meetups that ensure your dog stays happy, healthy, and well-socialized.
