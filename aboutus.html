About us

Because we love to talk about ourselves (and our dogs)

At Happy Tails, we bring dog owners together to find playmates, trusted pet care, and a supportive community. Whether you're looking for a fun playdate or a reliable dog sitter, we make it easy to connect with like-minded owners who love their dogs as much as you do. Because every pup deserves friendship, care, and a happy tail!

Our mission!

Our mission is to create a vibrant and trusted community where dog owners can connect, socialize, and find playmates, care, and friendship for their furry companions. We make every dog's life happier and healthier by fostering meaningful connections and offering convenient services like playmates and dog-sitting, because every pup deserves a friend!
